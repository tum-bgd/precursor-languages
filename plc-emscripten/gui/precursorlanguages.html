Precursor Languages for C

Algorithmic Thinking

Welcome

This application aims at teaching computational thinking and preparing to learn the language C based on ideas including, but not limited to NIKI and Karel the Robot.

Interactive Mode

  • Click on Interactive...
  • If wanted, modify and load the world in The World...

Programming Mode

  • First, open the tab "The World" and load a world for our robot.
  • Then, you will be on the tab "The Robot" and can steer the robot with its two operations.

Educational Material

  • Educational material (lectures, tasks, tutorials,...) are in preparation and presented as part of Computational Foundations at Technical University of Munich

Code Documentation & Contribution

  • ... will become possible on github: https://github.com/tum-bgd/precursor-languages
